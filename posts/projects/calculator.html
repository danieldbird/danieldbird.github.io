Calculator

1st March 2022

Source Code | Live Demo

The Project

As part of our coursework for Enspiral Dev Academy, one of our stretch material challenges was to participate in pair-programming.

My dev-academy team-mate Chen Yao had said previously that she would be keep to partner up for this challenge, so we went ahead and tackled this project together.

The brief was to make a functioning calculator.

We took this a step further and made it so the calculator could perform mutiple sums at once, and in order of operations.

Communication

To work seamlessly together we looked at what could be the best way to collaborate on the project in real-time. This had to be done online.

At first we tried Repl.it, but found it slow to work in.

We then looked at a VSCode extension called Live Share.

Although we could not get the voice audio to work through this extension, the ability to see each others cursor in real-time, AND be in the editor we use day-to-day with our favourite VSCode extensions and shortcuts, made this our method of choice.

To overcome the lack of voice, we utilised Slack calls, as that is where we were communicating via chat anyway.
